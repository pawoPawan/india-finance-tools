📊

Upload Zerodha, Groww, Schwab or Vested export files. Instantly get STCG/LTCG breakdown, FIFO cost basis, and ITR-ready summaries.

MF Screener & Analytics

Screen 13,000+ Indian mutual funds by returns, expense ratio, and composite score. Drill into holdings and find funds by the stocks you want.

Why We Built This

Most finance tools in India either require you to create an account, upload sensitive data to a server, or pay a subscription fee for basic features.

We believe the tools you use to manage your money should work entirely on your device. No servers see your broker statements. No one tracks what stocks you own. Your financial data is yours.

Everything runs in your browser — calculations, data processing, even the mutual fund database is cached locally. The only network calls are to fetch publicly available fund data from Morningstar India.
