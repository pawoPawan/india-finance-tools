Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Your financial data never leaves your device. We use cookies only for Google AdSense (ads) and analytics. You can opt out at any time.

1. Who We Are

br27.in ("we", "us", "our") is a personal finance tools website for Indian investors. The site is operated independently.

Contact: hello@br27.in

2. What Data We Collect

Data you provide: None. We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal information.

Data stored locally on your device (not sent to us):

  • Mutual fund data downloaded from Morningstar India (stored in your browser's IndexedDB)
  • Your uploaded broker files (Capital Gains Calculator — processed entirely in your browser, never uploaded)
  • App preferences and settings (stored in your browser's localStorage)

Data collected automatically (by third-party services):

  • Your IP address and browser information — collected by Google AdSense and Google Analytics when you visit this site
  • Pages you visit and time spent — collected by Google Analytics
  • Ad interactions (clicks, impressions) — collected by Google AdSense

3. Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to understand how visitors use our site
  • Preferences: We store your cookie consent choice in your browser's localStorage

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Some features (like personalised ads) will not work without cookies.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects data anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Your Financial Data

All financial data you work with on this site — including uploaded broker files, calculated capital gains, and mutual fund data — is processed entirely within your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party (except as required to fetch publicly available fund data from Morningstar India via our CORS proxy).

7. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain. Data stored in your browser (IndexedDB, localStorage) can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian and international privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access information about what data is collected about you (via Google's tools)
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Delete locally stored data (via your browser settings)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Third-Party Links

Our tools access publicly available data from Morningstar India. We are not affiliated with Morningstar. Please review Morningstar's own privacy policy for information on their data practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@br27.in.
